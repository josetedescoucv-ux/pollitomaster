# Pollito Master

## Vision

Pollito Master is a desktop-first productivity platform designed to help people organize their time, attention, and responsibilities with clarity and calm.

It exists to make planning feel lighter, more reliable, and more human. Instead of forcing people to manage their lives through fragmented tools, Pollito Master brings scheduling, reminders, priorities, and daily focus into one coherent experience.

It solves the common problem of modern overload: too many tasks, too many systems, and not enough clarity about what matters right now. Pollito Master is built to help users regain control of their day without feeling overwhelmed by complexity.

## Target users

Pollito Master is designed for people who need dependable structure in their daily lives, including:

- Professionals who manage deadlines, meetings, and priorities across multiple responsibilities.
- Freelancers who balance client work, personal projects, and irregular schedules.
- Students who need a calm system for coursework, deadlines, and study planning.
- People with ADHD or executive function difficulties who benefit from clear, low-friction planning tools.
- Anyone who needs reliable scheduling support and a more intentional way to manage time.

## Core principles

Pollito Master is guided by a simple set of product principles:

- Fast: the experience should feel immediate and efficient.
- Beautiful: the product should feel refined, calm, and trustworthy.
- Reliable: users should feel they can depend on the system when planning their day.
- Offline-first: essential planning should remain useful even without a constant connection.
- Keyboard-friendly: power users should be able to work efficiently without relying on the mouse.
- Minimal friction: the product should reduce effort rather than add overhead.
- Data belongs to the user: privacy, ownership, and transparency matter.

## User experience philosophy

The experience should feel calm, deliberate, and focused on helping users act with confidence.

Core experience principles include:

- Every important action should take as few clicks as possible.
- The planner should never feel cluttered or visually noisy.
- Typography should be large, readable, and comfortable for long sessions.
- Excellent keyboard shortcuts should make power usage feel natural and efficient.
- Comfortable spacing should create a sense of clarity and ease.
- Progressive disclosure should be used so advanced options remain hidden until needed.
- The product should feel supportive rather than demanding.
- The interface should encourage calm focus rather than constant stimulation.

## Desktop-first philosophy

The desktop application is the primary experience because it offers the best environment for deep planning, sustained focus, and rich productivity workflows.

A desktop experience allows users to:

- view more information at once
- manage complex schedules with greater clarity
- work comfortably for extended sessions
- use keyboard-driven workflows efficiently
- keep a persistent workspace for planning and execution

The Android companion app will be intentionally focused and lightweight. Its purpose is not to replace the desktop experience, but to extend it into everyday mobility. The companion app will prioritize:

- alarms
- quick capture
- viewing today's schedule
- simple edits

This ensures the mobile experience stays useful without becoming bloated or overly complex.

## Long-term modules

Pollito Master is designed as a platform with room for multiple connected product areas over time.

### Planner
A central space for organizing tasks, priorities, schedules, and focus time.

### Calendar
A clear view of appointments, deadlines, and recurring commitments.

### Smart Alarms
A thoughtful alarm experience designed to support routines, wake-ups, and time-based reminders.

### AI Assistant
A helpful companion that can support planning, summarizing, and decision-making without replacing the user's judgment.

### Goals
A place to define meaningful outcomes and track progress over time.

### Habits
A module for building consistency through repeatable routines and small daily actions.

### Notes
A lightweight space for capture, reflection, and supporting context.

### Statistics
Insights into productivity patterns, focus consistency, and progress over time.

### Settings
A place to manage preferences, privacy, integrations, and personalization.

## Product position

Pollito Master aims to be a dependable productivity platform for people who want structure without friction. It should feel like a calm professional tool that supports real life rather than adding another layer of complexity.

The long-term vision is to create a product that helps users plan better, follow through more easily, and feel more in control of their time.
